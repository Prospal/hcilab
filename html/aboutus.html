Welcome to Golden Circle

For over half a century our House has represented the rarest gemstones. Generations of the Golden Circle have employed their expertise and experience, personally selecting every stone and-uniquely among diamond houses-presiding over every step of its journey. No stage is bypassed. No shortcuts are taken. Only perfection matters.

Only perfection

The task of our cutters and polishers requires astonishing dexterity, precision and patience.

Golden Circle is a famous jewelry store established in 1847. There are a lot of branches all over the world. Our store provides high-quality jewelry.
